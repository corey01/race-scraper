How to arrive

How to arrive

The route to the circuit from Melbourne airport is via the Tullamarine Freeway, which connects to the City Link. Follow the signs to the Monash Freeway then South Gippsland Hwy, Bass Hwy to San Remo then cross the bridge on to Phillip Island. Follow the Phillip Island Tourist Road signs, turning off at Back Beach Road where the circuit is located.
